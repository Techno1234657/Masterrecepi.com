1. Tea

Steps to make a Tea

For 2 people

  1. Put 1/2 Cup of Water In a Kettle or Tea pot
  2. Put 1 teaspoon Sugar
  3. Put 1 teaspoon Tea
  4. Put some Ginger
  5. Let it Boil
  6. Put 1/2 Cup Milk
  7. Let it Boil
  8. Strain it in 2 Cups

    9. Your Tea is Ready


2. Maggi

Steps to make Maggi

For 2 people

  1. Put 3 glass of Water in a Kadai
  2. Put 2 Maggi
  3. Take a bowl and add Maggi Masala
  4. Put 1/2 tea spoon Salt
  5. Put 1/2 tea spoon Haldi
  6. Put 1/2 teaspoon Powdered Mirchi
  7. Put Kashmiri Lal Mirch Powder as per your needs
  8. Put the Masala we made in Kadai
  9. Let the Water evaporate

    10. Enjoy your Maggi
